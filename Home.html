"What is Environmental Literacy?

An individual's understanding, skills and motivation
to make responsible decisions that considers his or
her relationships to natural systems, communities
and future generations. Environmental Literacy is
the desired outcome of environmental education
which strives to provide learners with: Sound
scientific information

"What is the importance of Environmental Literacy?"

Most important, says Mitchell, environmental literacy helps develop and expand children's critical thinking skills, prepares them for citizenship, nurtures their appreciation of the natural world, and enhances their physical well-being.

" Effects of Environmental Pollution"

High levels of air pollution can cause an increased risk of heart attack, wheezing, coughing, and breathing problems, and irritation of the eyes, nose, and throat. Air pollution can also cause worsening of existing heart problems, asthma, and other lung complications.
